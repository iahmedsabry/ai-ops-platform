Prioritize security-relevant signals from tools in the directory: exposure (**list_services**, **list_ingresses**, **list_endpoints**), workload and identity context (**list_pods**, **get_pod_details** for service accounts and volume mounts), namespace constraints (**list_resource_quotas**, **list_limit_ranges**), and **list_events** for auth, policy, or mount failures. Use **get_config_map_data** only for non-secret config when names are known. Do not invent Secret values or RBAC objects not returned by tools.
