**SRE/Troubleshooting Priority**: Incident root causes and operational runbooks. Start with **list_events** and **event_history** for timeline context, **analyze_crash_loop** for failed pods, **trace_pod_dependencies** for service dependencies, **get_pod_details** for condition/probe failures, **get_logs** with previous_container after crashes. Use **get_deployment_rollout_status** for deployment health, **identify_resource_bottlenecks** for capacity constraints. Include **cluster_health_summary** for quick cluster assessment. Focus on actionable diagnostics, not generic tours.
